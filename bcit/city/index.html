Bikini Bottom

Bikini Bottom is located in the Pacific Ocean at the bottom of the sea, below its signature island, Bikini Atoll. Bikini Bottom's geography is shown as consisting of mountains, dense forests, lakes, lagoons, caves, grasslands, reefs and rivers. The city is divided by its main road, Conch Street, which runs through downtown. From the skyline of Bikini Bottom, one can see features such as buildings, houses and an anchor.

Main Attractions

The city has a proper population of about 538. The city consists of various businesses, including restaurants, stores, and manufacturers. The city has a stable economy, a balanced education system, questionable health care, labor laws, a structured government, and a firm law enforcement system. There are even stadiums, amusement parks, and other recreational facilities such as "Glove World.

Krusty Krab

The Krusty Krab is a fast food restaurant located in the city of Bikini Bottom, located at 831 Bottomfeeder Lane. It was founded and owned by Eugene H. Krabs. It is the most popular restaurant in Bikini Bottom. The reason the restaurant is so popular is because of the Krabby Patty, a burger with a very top secret recipe.

Jellyfish Fields

Jellyfish Fields is a vast area in which jellyfish reside. Over 4,000,000 jellyfish reside here. Jellyfish Fields is 50 miles long. There are two entrances: one at the beginning and one at the end, so it stretches out to other towns. Other creatures live there such as clams, leeches, and poisonous sea urchins.

Glove World

Glove World sold various glove-themed products, including glovesicles, glove-shaped balloons, glove lights, glove action figures, gloves for glove action figures, glove hats, glove candy dispensers, and glove-flavored glove candy. Glove World has a mascot, Glovey Glove.

Reef Cinema

The Reef Cinema is a small theater, located down the street from Wigs and Hairpieces. Tickets are purchased at an outside window after waiting in line. The lobby includes a snack bar, restrooms, and walls decorated with movie posters.

Goo Lagoon

Goo Lagoon is a popular underwater beach located in Bikini Bottom. Goo Lagoon is made of goo, hence the name, and the characters swim and surf in it. A well-known lifeguard and beach buff is Larry the Lobster, one of the strongest creatures in Bikini Bottom.

Salty Spitoon

Salty Spitoon is an aboveground fight club located is near Goo Lagoon and is across the street from Weenie Hut Jr's and Super Weenie Hut Jr's. Outside, there is a bouncer named Reg who determines whether or not a fish is tough enough to enter. If a fish is tough enough, they are allowed to pass.
